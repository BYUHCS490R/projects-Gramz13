Structural Tags (Header, Nav, Main, Footer)

  • What goes in the header? — are navigation links, identifier of what the website is all about such as Logo or website name, a search bar, and a guide.
  • What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website? — Links, Yes, Nav can go in the header. It doesn't have to go in the header, but you can also put it after the header. Nav should not contain links to a different website.
  • What goes in Main? — All the content or the main thing that the webpage is all about.
  • What goes in the Footer? — Copyright notice, Social Media Links, Contact Information, Legal links, Privacy Policy, or credits etc.
